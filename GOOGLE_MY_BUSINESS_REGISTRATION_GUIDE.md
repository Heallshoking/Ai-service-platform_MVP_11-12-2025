## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: Verification Postcard Not Received
**Solutions:**
- Wait up to 2 weeks for delivery
- Check with postal service
- Request a new postcard after 2 weeks
- Verify address is correct

### Issue: Business Already Listed
**Solutions:**
- Claim the existing listing
- Contact Google support if you can't claim it
- Merge duplicate listings

### Issue: Category Not Available
**Solutions:**
- Choose the closest available category
- Use "Other" if necessary
- Contact Google support for category requests

### Issue: Address Not Found
**Solutions:**
- Verify address spelling and format
- Use a more general address
- Contact Google support for assistance
