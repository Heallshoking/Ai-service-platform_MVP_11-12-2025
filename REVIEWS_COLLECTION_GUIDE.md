# ⭐ Reviews Collection Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to collect the first 5 reviews for your business on Google Maps, Yandex Maps, and 2GIS to improve your local search visibility and build trust with potential customers.

## Prerequisites
- Registered and verified profiles on:
  - Google My Business
  - Yandex Directory (Sprav)
  - 2GIS
- List of satisfied customers who can leave reviews
- Template messages for requesting reviews

## Step-by-Step Review Collection Process

### 1. Prepare Review Request Templates

#### Russian Template:
"Здравствуйте! Мы рады, что смогли помочь вам с [описание услуги]. Если вам понравилось качество нашей работы, будем благодарны за положительный отзыв на Google Maps, Яндекс.Карты или 2GIS. Это поможет другим клиентам найти нас быстрее. Спасибо!"

#### English Template:
"Hello! We're glad we could help you with [service description]. If you liked the quality of our work, we'd be grateful for a positive review on Google Maps, Yandex Maps, or 2GIS. This will help other customers find us faster. Thank you!"

### 2. Identify Potential Reviewers
1. Recent customers who expressed satisfaction
2. Long-term clients
3. Friends and family who have used your services
4. Colleagues in the industry

### 3. Contact Reviewers
1. Send personalized messages via:
   - Phone calls
   - SMS
   - WhatsApp
   - Email
   - Social media

2. Be polite and non-pushy:
   - "We hope you're happy with our service"
   - "If you have a moment, we'd appreciate your feedback"
   - "Your opinion helps us improve"

### 4. Provide Clear Instructions

#### For Google Maps Reviews:
1. Open Google Maps on mobile device or computer
2. Search for "БАЛТСЕТЬ Калининград"
3. Click on your business listing
4. Click "Написать отзыв" (Write a review)
5. Rate 4-5 stars
6. Write a brief description of service
7. Click "Опубликовать" (Publish)

#### For Yandex Maps Reviews:
1. Open Yandex Maps on mobile device or computer
2. Search for "БАЛТСЕТЬ Калининград"
3. Click on your business listing
4. Click "Написать отзыв" (Write a review)
5. Rate 4-5 stars
6. Write a brief description of service
7. Click "Опубликовать" (Publish)

#### For 2GIS Reviews:
1. Open 2GIS app or website
2. Search for "БАЛТСЕТЬ Калининград"
3. Click on your business listing
4. Click "Написать отзыв" (Write a review)
5. Rate 4-5 stars
6. Write a brief description of service
7. Click "Опубликовать" (Publish)

## Review Collection Strategies

### Strategy 1: Post-Service Follow-Up
1. Within 24-48 hours after completing work
2. Send thank you message
3. Request review if customer seems satisfied

### Strategy 2: In-Person Requests
1. At the end of service appointment
2. Ask satisfied customers directly
3. Provide business card with review instructions

### Strategy 3: Email Campaign
1. Send follow-up email after service completion
2. Include links to all review platforms
3. Make it easy to leave feedback

### Strategy 4: Loyalty Program Integration
1. Offer small discount for future services
2. In exchange for honest review
3. Make it a win-win situation

## Review Quality Guidelines

### Good Review Characteristics:
- Specific mention of services received
- Personal experience description
- Mention of positive aspects (quality, professionalism, punctuality)
- 4-5 star rating
- Authentic language

### Example Good Review:
"Заказывал замену розеток в квартире. Мастер приехал вовремя, быстро и качественно выполнил работу. Очень доволен результатом! Рекомендую!"

### What to Avoid:
- Asking for fake or dishonest reviews
- Offering payment for positive reviews
- Pressuring customers
- Generic or copied reviews

## Handling Negative Reviews

### Response Strategy:
1. Respond quickly (within 24 hours)
2. Acknowledge the issue
3. Apologize sincerely
4. Offer to resolve the problem
5. Take conversation offline if needed

### Example Response:
"Благодарим вас за обратную связь. Нам жаль, что вы остались недовольны. Пожалуйста, свяжитесь с нами по телефону [номер], чтобы мы могли исправить ситуацию. Ваше мнение важно для нас."

## Monitoring and Engagement

### Regular Monitoring:
1. Set up notifications for new reviews
2. Check all platforms daily
3. Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
4. Track review count and average rating

### Engagement Best Practices:
1. Thank customers for positive reviews
2. Show appreciation with personalized responses
3. Use reviews as testimonials (with permission)
4. Address concerns in negative reviews professionally

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: Customers Don't Know How to Leave Reviews
**Solutions:**
- Provide step-by-step instructions
- Send screenshots or video guides
- Offer to help over the phone
- Simplify the process

### Issue: Negative Reviews
**Solutions:**
- Respond professionally and quickly
- Offer to resolve the issue
- Learn from feedback
- Improve service quality

### Issue: Low Review Volume
**Solutions:**
- Increase outreach efforts
- Improve timing of requests
- Make review process easier
- Focus on most satisfied customers

## Best Practices

### Timing:
- Request reviews shortly after service completion
- Avoid weekends and holidays
- Consider customer convenience

### Personalization:
- Reference specific services provided
- Mention customer by name
- Customize message tone

### Persistence:
- Follow up once if no response
- Don't be pushy
- Respect customer decisions
